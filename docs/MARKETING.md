# Thina CRM — Marketing & Feature Guide

**Version:** 0.11.0 | **Last Updated:** April 2026

---

## The Name: "Thina"

**Thina** (pronounced *tee-nah*) is a Zulu/Xhosa word meaning **"us"** or **"we"**. In the South African real estate industry, success is never a solo achievement — it takes an agent, a buyer, a seller, a conveyancer, a bond originator, and an agency working together. Thina CRM embodies this spirit of collaboration: it is the platform where every relationship, every deal, and every interaction comes together as one.

---

## Purpose & Mission

Thina CRM exists to solve a specific problem: **South African estate agents do not have a CRM built for how they actually work.**

International CRMs don't understand mandates, FICA compliance, POPIA consent, show days, Property24 lead injection, BulkSMS, or the 9-stage OTP-to-commission pipeline that defines every South African property transaction. Generic tools force agents into workarounds — spreadsheets for commissions, WhatsApp for follow-ups, notebooks for show day visitors.

**Thina CRM replaces all of that with a single, purpose-built platform designed from day one for the South African real estate market.**

Our mission: **Give every estate agent in South Africa professional-grade tools to manage leads, close deals faster, stay compliant, and grow their business — without the complexity or cost of enterprise software.**

# 1. For Agency Principals & Management

> *"See everything. Control everything. Grow everything."*

## 1.1 Features & Benefits

### Pipeline Visibility Across Your Entire Agency

| Feature | What It Does | Benefit to Management |
|---------|-------------|----------------------|
| **Executive Dashboard** | Real-time KPI cards: Total Leads, Won Deals, Won Revenue, Pipeline Value, Active Transactions, Pending/Expected/Earned Commission | Instant snapshot of agency health without asking anyone for reports |
| **Pipeline Forecast** | Weighted pipeline value calculated by deal stage probability (New 10% → Proposal 65% → Won 100%) | Accurate revenue forecasting for cash flow planning |
| **Transaction Forecast** | Stage-weighted commission forecast across all active transactions (OTP Signed 30% → Commission Paid 100%) | Know exactly how much commission is in the pipeline and when it's likely to land |
| **Revenue Charts** | Visual pipeline waterfall, lead source distribution, status breakdown, and trend analysis | Spot patterns — which lead sources convert, which stages stall, where to invest |

### Lead & Deal Management at Scale

| Feature | What It Does | Benefit to Management |
|---------|-------------|----------------------|
| **Lead Scoring (0–100)** | Automated 5-dimension scoring: Deal Value, Pipeline Stage, Activity Engagement, Task Completion, Data Completeness | Objectively identify your hottest leads — no more gut-feel prioritisation |

| **Inbound Lead Capture** | Leads from Property24 and Private Property portals captured, parsed, and queued for agent review | Never lose a portal lead again — every enquiry is tracked from the moment it arrives |
| **Speed-to-Lead Rules** | Auto-response SMS triggered when a new lead arrives, a portal enquiry comes in, or a show day visitor registers | Respond in seconds, not hours — the agent who responds first wins the mandate |

### Compliance & Risk Management

| Feature | What It Does | Benefit to Management |

| **Document Vault** | Categorised document storage (FICA, OTP, Mandate, Bond, Transfer) per transaction and contact | Every document in one place — no more hunting through emails and filing cabinets |

# 4. Upcoming Features & Roadmap

> *"What's coming next — and why it matters."*

Thina CRM is under active development. Here's what's on the roadmap, with expected benefits for agencies, agents, and clients.

---

## 4.1 Near-Term (Next 1–2 Releases)

### WhatsApp Business Integration

**What it is:** Send and receive WhatsApp messages directly from Thina CRM. Template messages for common communications (viewing confirmations, offer updates, document requests). Read receipts and delivery status tracking.

**Benefits:**
- **For Management:** Centralised communication audit trail — see every client interaction across SMS and WhatsApp in one place
- **For Agents:** Communicate on the channel your clients actually use (90%+ of South Africans use WhatsApp daily) without leaving the CRM
- **For Clients:** Receive professional updates on WhatsApp — viewing confirmations, offer status, document requests — all from one verified number

### Live BulkSMS API Integration

**What it is:** Full integration with BulkSMS.co.za for real SMS delivery. Currently, the SMS interface is built and ready; this release connects it to the live BulkSMS REST API for actual message sending and delivery receipts.

**Benefits:**
- **For Management:** Automated speed-to-lead responses go live — every portal enquiry gets an instant SMS reply
- **For Agents:** Send real SMS from the CRM — follow-up sequences execute automatically, not just as reminders
- **For Clients:** Receive timely, professional SMS communications — confirmations, reminders, and updates

### Automated Portal Webhook (Property24 / Private Property)

**What it is:** A secure API endpoint that receives leads directly from property portals via email webhook (SendGrid Inbound Parse). No more manual paste-and-parse — leads flow in automatically.

**Benefits:**
- **For Management:** Zero-touch lead ingestion — every portal enquiry enters the CRM within seconds, 24/7
- **For Agents:** Leads appear in your inbox automatically — no more checking email, copying, and pasting
- **For Clients:** Your enquiry is received and responded to faster than any manual process could achieve

### Firebase Storage for Document Uploads

**What it is:** Full file upload capability in the Document Vault. Currently, document metadata is tracked; this adds actual file storage via Firebase Storage with secure access controls.

**Benefits:**
- **For Management:** All agency documents (FICA, OTP, mandates, bond letters) stored securely in the cloud with access control
- **For Agents:** Upload documents directly from your phone or desktop — drag-and-drop into any transaction
- **For Clients:** Your sensitive documents are stored with enterprise-grade security, not in email attachments

---

## 4.2 Medium-Term (3–6 Months)

### Lightstone Property Data Integration

**What it is:** Integration with Lightstone, South Africa's leading property data provider. Auto-populate property valuations, recent comparable sales, suburb price trends, and market analytics directly in Thina CRM.

**Benefits:**
- **For Management:** Data-driven pricing guidance for your agents — reduce the risk of over- or under-pricing mandates
- **For Agents:** Access property valuations and comparables without leaving the CRM; present professional CMA reports to sellers
- **For Clients:** Your agent gives you pricing based on actual market data, not guesswork — recent sales in your street, suburb trends, and realistic valuations

### Buyer-Property Auto-Matching Engine

**What it is:** The buyer profile system already captures preferences (budget, area, bedrooms, property type). This release adds an intelligent matching algorithm that automatically suggests properties to buyers when new listings arrive, and suggests buyers to agents when new mandates are signed.

**Benefits:**
- **For Management:** Faster deal flow — properties are matched to qualified buyers within hours of listing, not weeks
- **For Agents:** Get a list of matching buyers the moment you sign a new mandate; get property suggestions the moment a buyer registers
- **For Clients:** You're notified about matching properties before they hit the portals — first-mover advantage

### Follow-up Sequence Execution Engine

**What it is:** The sequence builder is already live. This release adds the execution engine that automatically sends SMS and email messages at scheduled intervals based on triggers (new lead, show day visit, proposal sent).

**Benefits:**
- **For Management:** Consistent follow-up across the agency — no agent "forgets" to follow up, the system does it automatically
- **For Agents:** Set it and forget it — enrol a lead in a sequence and the CRM handles the rest while you focus on closings
- **For Clients:** You receive professional, timely follow-ups — not a barrage on day one and then silence

## 4.3 Long-Term Vision (6–12 Months)

### Multi-Agent & Team Management

**What it is:** Role-based access control (principal, team leader, agent), team dashboards, lead assignment and routing, performance leaderboards, and agency-wide reporting.

**Benefits:**
- **For Management:** Assign leads to agents, set targets, view team performance, and manage access — true agency management
- **For Agents:** See your personal metrics alongside the team; receive assigned leads directly in your pipeline
- **For Clients:** Enquiries routed to the right agent for your area and property type — faster, more relevant service

### Commission Payout & Invoicing

**What it is:** Generate commission invoices, track payout status, calculate franchise fees and VAT obligations, and export to accounting software (Xero/Sage).

**Benefits:**
- **For Management:** Automated commission calculations, split tracking, and invoice generation — eliminate month-end admin
- **For Agents:** See exactly when your commission will be paid; generate professional invoices in one click
- **For Clients:** Transparent fee structures — know exactly what the commission covers and how it's calculated

### Mobile App (iOS & Android)

**What it is:** A native mobile application providing full Thina CRM functionality optimised for on-the-go estate agents.

**Benefits:**
- **For Management:** Your team has CRM access everywhere — no excuses for not logging activities or updating deals
- **For Agents:** Full CRM in your pocket — update deals from a show day, log a call from your car, check your pipeline anywhere
- **For Clients:** Your agent is always up to date, no matter where they are — no "I'll check when I'm back at the office"

### AI-Powered Insights

**What it is:** Machine learning models trained on your agency's historical data to predict deal outcomes, recommend next-best actions, identify at-risk deals, and optimise lead routing.

**Benefits:**
- **For Management:** Predictive analytics — know which deals will close and which need intervention before it's too late
- **For Agents:** AI recommends what to do next: "Call this lead — they've gone quiet for 5 days and deals at this stage have a 40% higher close rate with a Day 5 call"
- **For Clients:** A more proactive, data-informed agent who reaches out at the right time with the right information
